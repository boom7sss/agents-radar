3. **Knowledge Graphs as the RAG Backbone** – `Graphify` (+1095 today) and `cognee` (27k) illustrate a clear trend: **codebases, documentation, and infrastructure are being modeled as graphs** to enable deeper retrieval than flat vector search. Combined with the rise of `mem0` and `LightRAG`, we see RAG evolving from simple chunk retrieval to **structured, memory‑augmented reasoning**. This is likely connected to recent LLM releases (e.g., Kimi‑K2.6 support in ollama) that demand richer context—graphs reduce token waste.
